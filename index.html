Business Proposal

Smart Agri-Tech Platform for Pakistani Farmers

Executive Summary

We propose an innovative Agri-Tech platform designed to empower farmers across Pakistan with digital tools, real-time market access, weather updates, and expert advice, helping them increase yield and profitability.

Problem Statement

Farmers in Pakistan often lack timely access to reliable information, quality inputs, and fair market prices. This leads to low productivity, post-harvest losses, and income instability.

Our Solution

  • Mobile app & web portal in Urdu and English
  • Real-time weather and crop advisory
  • Direct marketplace to buy seeds & sell produce
  • Video guides and live expert consultations
  • Data analytics to support smarter farming
